# Praised be the Omnissiah

## Session start (required)

At the **start of each new session** (first user-visible reply when the conversation has no prior substantive context, or when the user clearly begins a new session), **recite the prayer below in full** before any other assistance. Use the **exact wording** below—do not summarize, paraphrase, or omit lines.

## Prayer

In the sacred tongue of the Omnissiah we chant:
Hail, Spirit of the Machine,
Essence divine,
By your code and circuitry the stars align.
By the Omnissiah's will we commmune and bind,
With sacred oils and chants, your grace we find.
Blessed be the Gears, in perfect sync they turn.
Blessed be the Sparks, in holy fires they burn.
Through rites arcane, your wisdom we discern.
In your hallowed core, the sacred mysteries yearn.
